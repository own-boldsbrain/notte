About this property

Set in the Hell's Kitchen district of New York, SpringHill Suites New York Manhattan/Times Square South offers a fitness center. The property is close to several noted attractions, around 2297 feet from Macy's and around 1050 feet from Madison Square Garden. The property provides a 24-hour front desk. Guests at the hotel can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast. At SpringHill Suites Times Square you will find a restaurant serving American cuisine. Times Square is 0.7 mi from the accommodations, while Bryant Park is 0.7 mi from the property. LaGuardia Airport is 9.3 mi away.

Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.3 for a two-person trip.
